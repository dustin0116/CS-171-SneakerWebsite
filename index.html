What are Sneakers?

Why are they important?

Sneakers are shoes, or items of footwear. They're something that's easily overlooked by people. People may view it as just
a simple piece of everyday wear, but sneakers mean so much more. Sneakers are important because it's a tool to help us
walk outside and also play different sports and exercises. Professional atheletes are very particular when it comes to sneakers,
as specific models and the technology affect how they perform.
